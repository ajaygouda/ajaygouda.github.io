My Roll

UI Design, UX Design, UI Develop



The Problem

Novelty discovered that stationery , art and craft enthusiasts were facing difficulty in finding the products all under one roof at competent prices.



The Task

To design a beautiful application where any type of user can easily access and buy stationary products.



Product Vision and Solution

A simple user interface for customers where everyone can easily access and buy 30000 stationary products.

A simple admin panel for client to manage products, generate reports, manage delivery products, mange, bulk order requests, manage bloggers, manage customers, area wise delivery mapping, sale management, sales return, notification master, knowledge master, category manage, brand manage, email master, payment history, specification master.



Research Method

As a starting point we each did some market research on competitors to investigate the current offering in the market and take inspiration from particular features that we liked about each website.



The Design Process

I have designed some mockups with matched color their existing logo and sent to client for review. After some version changes they decided go with these final design.
